Description

For this project, the goal was to create a website that will allow students to submit research projects that the faculty and admin can look at. Once submitted the form will show up on the admin's page along with all the other forms. The forms that show up on the admin's page can be viewed, edited, and filtered out depending on their status. The admin can deny or approve the forms which change their status. If they approve it, they will send it to the appropriate faculty for their approval. Once it is signed, the process is completed and the student is approved to take the course with the submitted project.
