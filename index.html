I've been a critical thinker for much of my life. No matter what question was presented to me and I always wanted to make sure I had the right answer for them. This especially occurred when I was in high school trying to decide what I wanted to pursue as my career in 2018. It wasn't until my sophomore year of college in 2020 when my professor was discussing careers that I had that answer. He began talking about data analysts and scientists while going over the type of work that they do and the way they think through their work. I found myself reeled into the discussion and felt the characteristics he mentioned fell in line with me. Afterward, I decided that this is what I was meant to do which gave me my drive. As I read up more on data analysts and scientists, I became more sure of my decision. I found what felt right for me and I am confident to say that this path is meant for me.
